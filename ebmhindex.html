The Rollerteam Motorhome is a truly versatile vehicle and is ideal for couples and families.
It can accommodate up to six people, with a huge fixed double berth above the cab, two large fixed bunk beds at the rear and another double berth that can be made up in the centre.
It has a shower and cassette toilet, with plenty of hot water produced by the gas boiler. Fully heated, this vehicle can be used all year round; great for ski trips.
The kitchen area has a three ring hob, oven and grill. The large fridge and freezer compartment runs off mains electricity, gas or battery power for when you are on the move. There are seat belts fitted in the dining area for four people. There is a large storage area under the bunk beds to store items such as a generator, bbq, chairs etc. The bike will take four bikes
